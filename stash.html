📦 How to Use

Stash Commands

Just tell Trizai to stash something. It is saved permanently in Memory/stash.md.

📥 Save to Stash

Say: Stash this: [item] with tag [tag]

🔍 View Stash

Say: Show my stash or What is in my stash?

🏷️ Filter by Tag

Say: Show stash tagged [tag]

🗑️ Unstash

Say: Archive stash #[number] or Unstash #[number]
